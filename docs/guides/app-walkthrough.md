# App Walkthrough

## Market Edge

Enter each outcome, bookmaker odds, and model probability. The app calculates
market probability, no-vig probability, model fair odds, edge, EV, and
fractional Kelly stake. This is the core line-shopping and value-betting view.

![Market edge](../assets/sports-edge-market-edge.png)

## Market Data

Use sample markets or public Polymarket data to bring external market
probabilities into the same framework. The comparison panel lets you test a
model probability against a selected market outcome.

![Market data](../assets/sports-edge-market-data.png)

## Football Poisson Model

Set home and away expected goals. The model creates a score distribution and
derives market probabilities for 1X2, goals, BTTS, and correct score. The edge
table compares those probabilities against book prices.

![Football Poisson](../assets/sports-edge-football-poisson.png)

## Model Evaluation

Upload predictions or use the sample log. The app reports Brier score, log loss,
ROI, hit rate, average edge, closing-line value, calibration, ROI by edge bucket,
and equity/drawdown curves.

![Model evaluation](../assets/sports-edge-model-evaluation.png)

## Methodology

The methodology tab is the project explanation layer. It documents the
workflow, formulas, assumptions, and why post-trade evaluation matters.

![Methodology](../assets/sports-edge-methodology.png)
